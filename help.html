🆘 Help & Support

Welcome to Sketch2Web – where your designs come to life as real, functional websites! Here’s everything you need to know to get started:

📌 What is Sketch2Web?

Sketch2Web allows you to upload hand-drawn or digital UI sketches, and it will generate corresponding HTML/CSS code automatically – helping you fast-track your web development process.

🚀 How to Use Sketch2Web

  1. Upload Your Sketch
    Make sure your sketch is clear and labeled (e.g., buttons, navbar, input fields).

  2. Click “Generate Code”
    Our AI processes the sketch and converts it into HTML/CSS.

  3. Preview
    View the generated web page.

  4. Copy Code
    Copy them to your clipboard.

💡 Tips for Better Results

  • Use dark markers or pens on white paper for best visibility.
  • Clearly label UI elements like buttons, navbars, and inputs.
  • Keep layouts neat and avoid overlapping components.
